What We Are

We are located at Unit 3c & 3d Killinarden Enterprise Park, Tallaght, Dublin 24. We started off as small business in a school doing bootcamps and then introduced different classes such as Trx. There was a strong clientele for this. After a few years we got a small studio and introduced Body-Tec classes. With the demand for the Body-Tec we transitioned into a much bigger studio where we are located now. We hold multiple classes six days a week. Some include Body-Tec, Fitness Classes and Personal Training. You can learn more about these classes in the sections above. Every training session focuses on body and mind. We don't judge anyone here. Our age groups are from people in their 20's up to people in their 50's. Everyone works at their own pace but we do encourage them to push themselves and we also push them to a certain extent of their own abilities.

Our trainers teach a mixture of different classes and implement different training methods into each class, so every training session is something different. Our trainers all hold fitness certificates and are 100% passionate about their jobs. Our trainers have a combined 23 year's experience which they all share with each other. Our trainer's motivate clients by setting realistic goals and providing feedback to clients.
